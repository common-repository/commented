== Description ==
Commented is a powerful feedback and collaboration tool designed to streamline communication within your team and enhance productivity. Easily collect, manage, and act upon feedback directly within your WordPress environment.
**Key Features:**
- **Streamlined Feedback:** Gather feedback on your content, design, or development directly within your WordPress dashboard. Say goodbye to scattered email threads and multiple platforms.
- **Task Management:** Convert feedback into actionable tasks. Commented seamlessly integrates with your workflow, allowing you to manage and track tasks effortlessly.
- **Real-Time Collaboration:** Foster collaboration with real-time discussions. Team members can discuss feedback, make decisions, and streamline the entire feedback-to-action process.
- **Integrations:** Connect Commented with your favorite tools. Currently supporting integrations with Notion, Slack, Linear, Zapier, and more. Explore the integration settings in the Commented dashboard.

== Installation ==
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
2. From the WordPress sidebar, navigate to Commented and click on the "Click here to activate" button.
3. Visit your website.
4. You will see a widget at the bottom center of your website.
5. Login or sign up for Commented by clicking the widget.
6. Simply click where you want to leave feedback.
7. Visit the Commented dashboard to see all comments and details.
== Frequently Asked Questions ==
**Q: How do I start using Commented?**
A: After activating the plugin, visit your WordPress website and log in to your Commented account. You can start commenting right away.
**Q: Can I integrate Commented with other tools?**
A: Yes, Commented offers integrations with popular tools like Notion, Slack, Linear, Zapier, and more. Explore the integration settings in the Commented dashboard to configure.
